Xavier Carreras

My research is in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I am interested in grammatical induction and parsing methods for syntactic-semantic analysis and translation of natural languages.

In 2005 I completed my PhD at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC) under the supervision of Lluís Màrquez. From 2006 to 2009 I was a postdoc at MIT/CSAIL with Michael Collins. From 2009 to 2014 I was a Ramón y Cajal researcher at the TALP Research Centre of UPC. From 2014 to 2017 I was a senior researcher at Xerox Research Centre Europe (which became Naver Labs Europe in mid 2017). In 2018 I joined the research team of dMetrics.

I am a research scientist at the Artificial Intelligence Research Institute (IIIA-CSIC) near Barcelona, since 2020.

Check my activities, my publications, and my profile on Google Scholar.
