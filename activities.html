Xavier Carreras: Activities

  • I participated in the following research projects: XLike, the Pascal2 Network, FAUST, MOLTO, OpenMT-2, KNOW-2
  • I was PC member of ACL-2015, NAACL-2015, CoNLL-2015, EMNLP-2014, CoNLL-2014, COLING-2014, ECAI-2014, EACL-2014, ACL-2013, NAACL-2013, EMNLP-2013, IWPT-2013, CoNLL-2013, ACL-2012, EMNLP-CoNLL-2012, ECML/PKDD-2012, ICML-2011, IJCAI-2011, CoNLL-2011, EMNLP-2011, ACL-2010, ICML-2010, CoNLL-2010, EMNLP-2010 COLING-2010, ECAI-2010, NIPS-2009, EACL-2009, NAACL/HLT-2009, IJCAI-2009, ... and many past editions of the same conferences
  • I was president of SIGNLL (2014-2016), the ACL Special Interest Group on Natural Language Learning. I was the secretary during 2011-2014.
  • I was program co-chair of EMNLP 2016 with Kevin Duh.
  • I taught Advanced Natural Language Processing in the AI Masters program of FIB.
  • I gave invited lectures at the 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014 and 2015 Lisbon Machine Learning School (LXMLS).
  • I was tutorial co-chair of EMNLP-2014.
  • I was co-organizer of the Eighth Workshop on Syntax, Semantics and Structure in Statistical Translation (SSST-8) at EMNLP-2014.
  • I was area chair of ACL-2014 for syntax, tagging, chunking and parsing, with Slav Petrov.
  • I co-organized the NIPS'12 Workshop on Cross-lingual Technologies (xLiTe)
  • I served as area chair for syntax and parsing for EACL 2012, with Kenji Sagae.
  • I served as area chair for syntax and parsing for ACL HLT 2011, with Ryan McDonald.
  • I co-chaired the demo track of ECML/PKDD 2010 with Ulf Brefeld.
  • I co-chaired CoNLL-2009 with Suzanne Stevenson
  • I was guest editor of Computational Linguistics : Special issue on Semantic Role Labeling (2008), with Lluís Màrquez, Ken Litkowski, and Suzanne Stevenson
  • In 2004 and 2005 I co-organized with Lluís Màrquez the CoNLL Shared Tasks on Semantic Role Labeling
